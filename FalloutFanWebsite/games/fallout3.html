Fallout 3

Overview

The game is set within a post-apocalyptic, open world environment of Washington, D.C. The main story takes place in the year 2277, around 36 years after the events of Fallout 2. Players take control of an inhabitant of Vault 101 who is forced to venture out into the Capital Wasteland to find their father after he disappears from the Vault under mysterious circumstances.

History in the market

Fallout 3 was met with critical acclaim and received a number of Game of the Year awards, praising the game's open-ended gameplay and flexible character-leveling system, and is considered one of the best video games of all time. Fallout 3 sold over 610,000 units during its initial month of release in October 2008, performing better than Bethesda's previous game, The Elder Scrolls IV.
